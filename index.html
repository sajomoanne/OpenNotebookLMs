OpenNotebookLMs: Discover & Share AI Research Notebooks

NotebookLM is incredible for private research, but it has no discovery layer. OpenNotebookLMs turns those private notebooks into a searchable, public gallery of AI‑augmented research.

Browse curated notebooks across various subjects and topics. Share your studies and research with the community, and learn from how others synthesize complex information with AI.
